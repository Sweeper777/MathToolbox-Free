Do you think doing maths and remembering all those complex formulas are just too hard? Then this app is for YOU!

In Math Toolbox Free, there are over 15 different operations that help your calculations. Ranging from circles to polygons, from trigonometry to sectors of a circle, and from factors to scientific notations. There must be a few that will come in handy!

Sounds overwhelming? The toolbox also provides a very helpful help screen. Just read through it and it will tell you how to use this app in great detail.

Still don't understand? Just download this app and try it out for yourself! The proof is in the eating!
